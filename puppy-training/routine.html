Sample daily routine

Structure prevents chaos. Puppies thrive on routine like toddlers thrive on nap schedules.

puppy being crazy right now? Check this schedule. What did you miss? A skipped nap or missed play session = demon mode. Or hit the panic button →
6:30 AM
Wake up → potty IMMEDIATELY Carry them outside if needed. Reward the second they go.
6:45 AM
Breakfast (hand-fed) Use meal for recall game or training. Food = learning tool, not just a bowl.
7:00 AM
Potty → short play 10-15 min of interaction. Then let them settle.
7:30 AM
Crate nap (1.5-2 hrs) They need SO much sleep. Enforced nap time is not cruel, it's necessary.
9:30 AM
Potty → training session (2-3 min) One skill. Keep it short and fun. End on a win.
10:00 AM
Supervised exploration / socialization New surface, new sound, new person. Brief and positive.
10:30 AM
Crate nap (1.5-2 hrs) Yes, another one. Puppies need 18-20 hrs of sleep per day.
12:30 PM
Potty → lunch (hand-fed or puzzle toy) More training reps with their meal.
1:00 PM
Play + enrichment Snuffle mat, Kong, tug. Engage their brain.
1:30 PM
Crate nap Sensing a pattern? Good.
3:30 PM
Potty → training (2-3 min) → play Practice a different skill than morning.
4:00 PM
Crate nap or supervised chill Capture calm if they're just hanging out.
5:30 PM
Potty → dinner (hand-fed) More recall game or capturing good behaviors.
6:00 PM
Family time / supervised play Practice calm greetings if people come home.
7:00 PM
Last potty → wind down → crate for bed Low energy evening. No hype before bed.
7:00 AM
Potty → breakfast → training (5 min) Longer sessions now. Work on recall, leave it, or impulse control.
7:30 AM
Walk (15-20 min) Practice loose leash. Reward check-ins. Let them sniff.
8:00 AM
Settle time / crate Still need enforced rest but can handle longer awake periods.
11:00 AM
Potty → enrichment / puzzle toy Mental stimulation session.
11:30 AM
Nap / crate 1-2 hours.
1:00 PM
Potty → lunch → training (5 min) Work a different skill than morning.
1:30 PM
Play session / socialization outing On/off switch practice. New environments.
2:30 PM
Settle time Practice place/mat training or just capture calm.
5:00 PM
Walk (20-30 min) + potty Longer walk. Practice recall on long line in safe area.
6:00 PM
Dinner → training → play Use dinner for the most challenging skill you're working on.
8:00 PM
Wind down → last potty → bed Calm evening. Chew toy in crate.
7:00 AM
Potty → breakfast → walk (30 min) Morning walk is the main exercise. Practice everything you've been working on.
8:00 AM
Training session (5-10 min) Proofing skills in different contexts. Generalize.
8:30 AM
Settle / alone time practice They should be able to chill while you work. Reward it.
12:00 PM
Midday potty → enrichment → lunch Puzzle feeder, snuffle mat, or scatter feeding in yard.
12:30 PM
Nap or supervised chill They still need rest periods even at this age.
4:00 PM
Afternoon walk/play (30-45 min) Higher energy activity. Fetch, play dates, long line recall practice.
5:30 PM
Dinner → training → wind down Use dinner calories for the hardest thing you're teaching right now.
8:00 PM
Evening chill → last potty → bed By now they should be settling into a predictable rhythm.
just so you know If your puppy is being a demon at 2pm, check this schedule. Did they miss a nap? Did they miss a training session? Structure prevents chaos. Your puppy isn't bad — they're just under-scheduled.

Your dog is not the problem. Your inconsistency is.
